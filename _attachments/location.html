To reach Burg Liebenzell, first reach the nearby city of Pforzheim by long distance Deutsche Bahn. From the Pforzheim Hauptbahnhof you will find available regional trains to Bad Liebenzell. These leave approximately every hour. Check the Deutsche Bahn for details. From the Bad Liebenzell main station, the walk to the castle should be about 20 minutes. If you need assistance, PLEASE let us know and we can arrange to pick you up from the main station.
