Pre-Meeting Notes

For the Statistics workshop given by Prof. Caldwell, there are some articles that should be read in preparation.
  • Phys. Rev. D 74, 092003 (2006)
  • Phys. Rev. D 83, 012004 (2011)
  • Eur. Phys. J. Plus (2012) 127: 24
Additionally, Prof. Caldwell is the author of the Bayesian Analysis Toolkit. You are encouraged to be familiar with this tool (perhaps even attempt to install it on your local machine and run through a few tutorials.

note: Internet access at Burg Liebenzell will be somewhat restricted. Please do not plan to transfer lots of data during the workshop and try to limit your internet usage to a minimum (email and other low-traffic uses) so as to keep the network available for all.
